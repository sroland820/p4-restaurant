About Us

Sushi Maru invites you to experience "kaiten dining" serving both traditional and creative sushi in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. Here at Sushi Maru we always strive to create an exceptional dining experience by providing the highest quality food available while being affordable, professional and attentive.
